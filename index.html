Common Cattle & Buffalo Breeds

Gir

Gir is a famous Indian breed known for its high milk production and distinctive curved horns.

Murrah

Murrah is a high-yielding buffalo breed from India, known for its jet black color and curved horns.

Sahiwal

Sahiwal is a well-known dairy cattle breed from Pakistan and India, recognized for its reddish-brown coat.

Jersey

Jersey cattle are small-sized dairy cows with a light brown coat, known for rich milk with high butterfat.

Jaffarabadi

Jaffarabadi buffalo is a heavy breed from India, recognized by its large size and massive curved horns.

Kankrej

Kankrej is a dual-purpose breed from India, known for drought resistance and good milk and draught power.

About This Application

This application allows farmers, breeders, and enthusiasts to upload images of cattle or buffaloes to identify their breed using advanced image recognition technology. Recognizing the breed helps in better management, breeding decisions, and improving productivity.

Simply upload a clear image of the animal, and the system will analyze the features to provide the most likely breed match. The breed information section provides details about common breeds to help you learn more.

Developed with Tailwind CSS, Font Awesome, and modern web technologies for a responsive and user-friendly experience.
